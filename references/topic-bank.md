# IELTS Topic Bank

Only use topics from this bank. IELTS does not test highly technical, controversial, or culturally specific topics.

---

## Reading — Passage 1 (Factual / Descriptive)

| Category | Approved Topics |
|---|---|
| **History** | Ancient civilisations · historical inventions · archaeological discoveries · traditional crafts · exploration |
| **Natural World** | Animal behaviour · plant species · geological formations · weather phenomena · conservation |
| **Process / How It Works** | Manufacturing processes · scientific methods · natural cycles · food production |
| **People / Biography** | Inventors · explorers · scientists · artists · philanthropists |

**Avoid:** Military history · political figures · religious topics · graphic medical content

---

## Reading — Passage 2 (Discursive / Social)

| Category | Approved Topics |
|---|---|
| **Education** | Learning methods · online education · early childhood development · literacy |
| **Urban Issues** | Public transport · housing · city planning · green spaces |
| **Technology & Society** | Social media · automation · digital privacy · remote work |
| **Environment** | Renewable energy · waste reduction · climate adaptation · sustainable agriculture |
| **Health & Wellbeing** | Nutrition · exercise · sleep · mental health · workplace wellness |
| **Work & Economics** | Gig economy · workplace trends · entrepreneurship · remote teams |
| **Culture & Travel** | Tourism · cultural preservation · museums · heritage sites |

**Avoid:** Political debates · controversial social issues · religious comparisons

---

## Reading — Passage 3 (Analytical / Abstract)

| Category | Approved Topics |
|---|---|
| **Psychology** | Memory · decision-making · perception · learning · creativity |
| **Cognitive Science** | Consciousness · language acquisition · intelligence · brain plasticity |
| **Philosophy of Science** | Scientific method · paradigm shifts · ethics of research |
| **Sociology** | Group behaviour · cultural evolution · social norms · identity |

**Avoid:** Pseudoscience · unproven medical claims · paranormal topics · overly technical neuroscience
